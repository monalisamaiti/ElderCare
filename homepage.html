About Us

Key features of our initiative

We are a non-profit organization aiming to help the elderly with the comfort of being in their homes. We encourage people to volunteer for resources and services and make every possible help available to senior citizens. Always remember that the elderly aren’t weak or a burden on society instead they are pot full of knowledge, wisdom, experience and have done their part for the country. Now it’s our responsibility to support them and be some kind of help to them. To know more, check out stories and updates from our partners, team, and supporters.

Our community volunteers are a huge part of our success and we plan to meet more of you. ElderCare services are specially designed to fulfil the needs and requirements exclusive to senior citizens. In ElderCare, the emphasis is on the personal and social requirements of the senior citizens.
Our ElderCare initiative is non-profit and aims to connect the volunteers with our members. We provide services from homemakers, chore workers, health aides, etc. Our volunteers can help them in their basic household tasks, like cleaning, running errands, meal preparation, personal care services, etc.
