Why your help is important to us?



Senior citizens living in poverty face abandonment by their own families as they cannot earn an income. They end up living alone and starving as they cannot afford even two meals a day. Donate to help the elderly and provide them with access to a safe old-age home where they can get food, shelter and medical care.

Those in extreme need are usually socially excluded and face a lack of necessities for reasons like living in underserved areas or the social stigma of being affected with an incurable illness. Donate to those in extreme need and help them with resources like food, medical care, livelihood resources, sanitation, education, etc. and enable them to lead a cheerful life.

Even the smallest of donations can make a big impact.
